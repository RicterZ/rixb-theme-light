light
================
A rixb theme

##Usage
Delete your rixb floder `static` and `templates`.

    $ cd rixb
    $ rm -Rf static
    $ rm -Rf templates

And copy this theme to your rixb floder.
	
	$ cd ..
	$ cp light/static rixb/static
	$ cp light/templates rixb/templates

And refresh in your borwser.
